About Us


Cutstistic is the realization of our dream! We are Norbert and Irene, two young locals who have been close friends for over nine years! Along the way we developed a mutual passion for casual restaurants and decided to embark on our new adventure to serve up a little something of our own.

We are happy to have you here at Cutstistic! Hope you enjoy not only the food and drinks, but also find a little time to relax, sit back with some good company and enjoy the atmosphere of our little refuge.

Thank you, very much, for visiting Cutstistic.

with love, Nor-Rene.
